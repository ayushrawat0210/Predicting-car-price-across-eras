The dataset will have features like:

1) Car_ID (Unique identifier)

2) Car_Name (Model name)

3) Brand (Car brand)

4) Year (Manufacturing year)

5) Category (Vintage, Modern, Luxury)

6) Price (Estimated price in USD)

7) Mileage (Miles per gallon or electric equivalent)

8) Engine_Size (Engine capacity in liters or equivalent)

9) Fuel_Type (Petrol, Diesel, Electric, Hybrid)

10) Transmission (Manual, Automatic, CVT, etc.)
